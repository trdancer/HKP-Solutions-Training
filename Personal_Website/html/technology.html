Troy is experienced in a wide range of different languages and technologies. At NYU he is a senior in Computer Science. There he has learned the key foundations to the art of programming. He is a self taught web developer and designer for both frontend and backend solutions.

He is currently the Technical Lead for the Strikethrough Score Project by Maxine Flasher-Düzgüneş, which explores the process of erasure in text as an interpretive score for dance.

Troy wishes to integrate his knowledge with programming and technology with dance and more generally the arts. Particularly he excited on how to bring technologies such as Machine Learning, Computer Vision, Procedural Generation, and Mobile Interactions to the arts.

Troy uses his creative side to bring innovative, and clean designs to digital experiences.
