About Dance Technology Other Contact

Troy has a BFA in Dance from NYU Tisch School of the Arts and is trained in contemporary, modern, improv, and ballet. He has choreographed for over 4 years and has taught dance for 2 years. Troy wishes to tell real, human narratives through his dance, ensuring there is always clarity, flavor, and poignancy.

In his work, Troy focuses on highly specific ideas, and how they are manifested through movement. Particularly, he works towards new ways of combining dance with technology and using his programming expertise to bring his ideas to life. Troy always strives to work collaboritvely with his fellow artists in visual design, music, and all other mediums.
